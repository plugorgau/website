July Talk: Raspberry Pi Jam

July 14, 2015

We will have a couple of short presentations form PLUG members that have done amazing things with their Pi’s followed by an informal breakout session where you can work on your latest project or discuss ideas.

Whether your new and looking for advice, a veteran looking to show off your creation, or maybe you have a Pi at home that’s gathering dust while you’re looking for inspiration come along and join us for a great night.

A Raspberry Pi is not required to attend but if you have one you are encouraged to bring it along. If you have a small SD or microSD card to spare or to share, anything from 128 MB and up will be useful, bring them too.
