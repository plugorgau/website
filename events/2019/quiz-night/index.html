Quiz Night, Belgian Beer Cafe, Perth CBD

November 19, 2019

The quiz questions aren’t technical - general knowledge is the game. It’s a social event, there’s prizes to be won, bring a few friends who may enjoy this kind of thing. We’ll make up tables of players on the night, we’re expecting 4 or 5 people to a table.

VENUE: Le Roi function room, Belgian Beer Cafe, Perth. Through to the beer garden, glass door at your right 347 Murray St PERTH WA 6000 https://www.belgianbeer.com.au/

TIME: Doors open 6:00 PM Quiz begins 7:00 PM.

FOOD: Light snacks provided, drinks and more food available from the main bar.

COST: Tickets at the door: $5 members & $10 for non-members. Membership renewal can be done there: $20/year and $10 concession. https://www.plug.org.au/membership/
